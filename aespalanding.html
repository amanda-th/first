about aespa

aespa (에스파) commonly stylized in all lowercase or æspa is a south korean girl group formed by sm entertainment.
the group consists of four members: karina, giselle, winter, and ningning. they are known for popularizing the metaverse concept and hyperpop music in k-pop. the members interact with their digital avatars, "ae", and experience new adventures.
